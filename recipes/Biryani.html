Biryani Recipe


Description

Prepare a marinated chicken using Ingredients:

  1. Paprika
  2. Salt
  3. Turmeric
  4. Biryani Masala
,and let it stand for 1hr to overnight depending on time left

Steps:

  1. Cook rice about 50% and keep it aside
  2. Layer the rice in a cooking utensil and layer a marinated chicken on top of rice layer. keep doing it until both rice and chicken are covered
  3. Heat it and close the pan tight so the hot air cant escape and create a hot temparature for well cooked rice
  4. After cooking it for around 30 minutes, Biryani is Done!
